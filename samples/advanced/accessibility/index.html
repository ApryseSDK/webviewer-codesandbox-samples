Instructions

Click Play or press p to play the text-to-speech feature. Press Tab to go to the next page.

The available controls are as follows:

  • Voice: Language and gender of the voice
  • Pitch: Pitch of the voice
  • Rate: Speed at which the text is read
  • Volume: Volume at which the text is read
